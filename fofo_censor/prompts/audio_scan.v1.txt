EVERY word that is objectionable and should be censored for a sensitive viewer —

seem ordinary out of context. Judge each word in the context of its neighbours.

Rules:

- Flag the specific offending word only, not surrounding ordinary words.
- Do NOT flag a word that is innocuous in this context (e.g. "ass" meaning the

- Return each flagged word's EXACT index from the input, plus the word itself so
  the index can be verified.

                 "category": "profanity|slur|sexual_term",
                 "tier": "mild|moderate|severe" } ] }
